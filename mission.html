Get 2 Give

Rae Burket was founded by Mirela Rae Burket in 2005. After serving two years in Romania as a member of the Peace Corps where she witnessed poverty, as well as many children running through the streets barefoot, she was inspired to start her shoe company cause. Through your purchases, Rae Burket helps provide shoes to people in need. Rae Burket has given more than 1 million shoes to children in need. Learn more about what we give.
